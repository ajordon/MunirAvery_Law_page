About Me


With an extensive background working in city and state government, Munir Avery has an excellent understanding of local, state and federal law as well as an in-depth knowledge and familiarity with Supreme Court, Surrogate's Court, Housing Court and Agency procedures and rules. Munir received his JD from Fordham Law School, his BA from SUNY Albany and a Regents Diploma from Cardozo High School.
